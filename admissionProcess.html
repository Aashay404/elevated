Admission Process

A thoughtful, personalized journey to ensure the best start for your child

How to Join

Your Child's Journey Begins Here

At Elevated Minds, we believe that every child is unique. Our admission process is designed to understand your child's individual needs and create a personalized learning path.

1

Initial Inquiry & Contact

Reach out to us via phone, email, or our contact form. Our friendly team will respond within 24 hours to understand your needs and answer any initial questions.

  • Fill out the inquiry form on our website
  • Call us at +91 9511 672 723
  • Email us at info@elevatedminds.com
  • Schedule a convenient time for a detailed discussion
2

Child Assessment

Our experienced team conducts a comprehensive assessment to understand your child's strengths, challenges, interests, and learning style.

  • Developmental screening and evaluation
  • Cognitive and behavioral assessment
  • Observation in interactive activities
  • Review of any existing reports or documentation
3

Parent Consultation

A detailed one-on-one meeting with parents to discuss assessment results, understand family goals, and address any concerns or questions.

  • Share assessment findings and insights
  • Discuss your child's specific needs and strengths
  • Understand your expectations and goals
  • Provide recommendations for appropriate programs
4

Enrollment Meeting

Once you decide to proceed, we'll guide you through the enrollment process, including paperwork, fee structure, and program details.

  • Complete enrollment forms and documentation
  • Review and sign service agreements
  • Discuss fee structure and payment options
  • Set start date and schedule preferences
5

Program Planning & Onboarding

Our team creates a customized program plan tailored to your child's unique needs, complete with goals, strategies, and a detailed roadmap.

  • Develop Individualized Education Plan (IEP)
  • Assign dedicated therapists/educators
  • Create session schedule and timeline
  • Orientation session for child and family
  • Introduction to staff and facilities
  • Set up progress tracking and communication

Documents Required for Enrollment

Child's birth certificate or age proof
Recent passport-size photographs (2 copies)
Parent/Guardian identification proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
Address proof (utility bill/rental agreement)
Previous medical or psychological reports (if available)
Previous school records or progress reports (if applicable)
Immunization records (recommended)

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the assessment take?
A: Typically 1-2 hours, depending on the child's age and needs.

Q: Is there a waiting list?
A: We try to accommodate all families promptly, usually within 1-2 weeks.

Q: Can I visit the facility before enrolling?
