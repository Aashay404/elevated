CCPF Partners with Local Clinics for Better Reach

CCPF Administration Feb 1, 2025 Partnership

New partnerships with healthcare providers across Nashik ensure more women have access to quality cervical cancer screening services.

The Cervical Cancer Prevention Foundation (CCPF) continues its mission to protect women's health by expanding access to early detection and preventive care. As part of this effort, CCPF has partnered with multiple local clinics and healthcare providers across Nashik, bringing cervical cancer screening services closer to the community.

This initiative marks an important step toward improving awareness, accessibility, and affordability of life-saving screening services for women.

Strengthening Community Healthcare in Nashik

Despite cervical cancer being highly preventable and treatable when detected early, many women still face challenges such as limited access to screening centers, lack of awareness, and social hesitation.

By collaborating with trusted local clinics, CCPF aims to:

  • Reduce the distance women need to travel for screening
  • Ensure timely and accurate Pap smear and HPV testing
  • Create a supportive, women-friendly healthcare environment

These partnerships enable CCPF to reach women in both urban and semi-urban areas of Nashik.

What These Partnerships Mean for Women

Through these collaborations, women can now access:

  • ✅ Quality cervical cancer screening services
  • ✅ Affordable or subsidized tests (where applicable)
  • ✅ Trained healthcare professionals following CCPF guidelines
  • ✅ Confidential and respectful care

Local clinics act as community touchpoints, making preventive healthcare more approachable and less intimidating.

Integrated Awareness and Screening Programs

Beyond testing, CCPF and its partner clinics will jointly conduct:

  • Community awareness sessions
  • Educational workshops on HPV and cervical cancer
  • Counseling on Pap smears and HPV vaccination
  • Referral and follow-up support when needed

This end-to-end approach ensures women not only get tested but also receive guidance and continuity of care.

A Collaborative Step Toward Early Detection

Early detection is the most powerful tool in preventing cervical cancer. With these new partnerships, CCPF can:

  • Increase screening rates
  • Identify pre-cancerous conditions early
  • Reduce late-stage diagnoses
  • Ultimately save lives

Together with local healthcare providers, CCPF is building a stronger preventive healthcare network in Nashik.

Looking Ahead

CCPF plans to continue expanding its clinic partnerships and outreach programs in the coming months. The goal is clear: no woman should miss life-saving screening due to lack of access or awareness.

Women are encouraged to contact participating clinics or visit CCPF's website to learn more about available screening services near them.
