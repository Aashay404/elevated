HPV Vaccination

Protect yourself against the types of Human Papillomavirus (HPV) that cause most cervical cancers. Safe, effective, and provides lifetime protection with just three doses.

  • Lifetime Protection in 3 Doses
  • Recommended for female (15–45 years)
  • Lifetime Protection in 2 Doses
  • Recommended for female (10–14 years)
  • Prevents majority of cervical cancers

Pap Smear Test

Detect early pre-cancerous changes in the cervix long before cancer develops. Quick, painless, and highly effective for early diagnosis and prevention.

  • Recommended for women age 21 years or married onwards
  • Screen every 2 years until age 65
  • Early detection saves lives
