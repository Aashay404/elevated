Delete Account

If you wish to delete your account from CCPF, you can request account deletion by contacting us via email.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once deleted, all your data will be removed from our system.

How to Request Account Deletion?

  • Send an email from your registered email ID
  • Use the subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Mention your registered mobile number or user details
Request via Email

Our team will verify your request and process it within 3–5 working days.
